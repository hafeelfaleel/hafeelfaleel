### ⚡ About this workshop

I build things until they work, break them until I understand why, then build them better. Right now that means a fully local, offline AI assistant — no cloud, no subscriptions, just a laptop and too much determination.

- 🔭 **Currently building:** [`Jarvis`](https://github.com/hafeelfaleel/Jarvis) — a local voice assistant powered by Whisper + Ollama
- 🌱 **Currently learning:** real-time audio processing, voice-activity detection, and making local LLMs feel instant
- 🎯 **Philosophy:** "It's not perfect, but it's mine" — v1 ships, then it gets better
- 💬 **Ask me about:** Python, local-first AI, CS50, or why I refuse to pay for cloud APIs when a laptop CPU is right there
